Ingredients:
  * chickpeas, 1 can or .75 cup dry, cooked
  * tahini, 1/4 cup
  * extra virgin olive oil, 2 TBSP
  * garlic, 2 cloves
  * lemon juice, 2 TBSP
  * salt
  * water, 1/4 cup

Steps:
  1. add tahini and olive oil to food processor, pulse to smooth
  1. add everything else, blend until smooth

Yields ~2 cups.
